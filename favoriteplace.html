During spring break while studying abroad in Spain, I decided to travel solo through Denmark and southern Sweden. My favorite stop on this trip was Aarhus, Denmark. While in Denmark's second-largest city, I visited an outdoor museum of real historical buildings from across Denmark dating back to the 17th century.
